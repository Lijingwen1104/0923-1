Technical and historical information

The delivery robots from Starship Technologies company began to replace some human couriers to deliver food. The small self-driving robot collects food from restaurants in Redwood City in California. Delivers meals to customers within 3.2 kilometers, and their robots are equipped with lidars and other sensors to navigate and interact with customers through touch screen and cameras. Nvidia AGX Xavier is equipped to process sensor and camera information in real time.
