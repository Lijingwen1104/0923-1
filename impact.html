Impact of Technology

The delivery robots has certain requirements for AI technology. It also needs to be equipped with various sensors, including multiple high resolution cameras and lidars, to sense the surrounding environment, locate, navigate and move in complex urban environments. It needs to recognize and avoid pedestrians, cars, traffic lights and signs in real time.

Advantage of delivery robots

  • 1.Robots can work in full time
  • 2.Large quantities of transported one time
  • 3.Intelligent planning of meal route
  • 4. Reducing labor costs

Disadvantages of delivery Robot

  • 1.humans may become overly dependent on the machines
  • 2.Robots cost much money in maintenance & repair
  • 3.people will lose their jobs
